Virgil Van Dijk

Dutch professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Premier League club Liverpool and captains the Netherlands national team.

Considered one of the best defenders in the world, I am known for my strength, leadership, and aerial ability. I am the only defender to win UEFA Men's Player of the Year, and has finished runner-up for the Ballon d'Or and Best FIFA Men's Player.
